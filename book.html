SONA Lab Bookings

Booking a Lab

In the Psychology department we use a system called SONA to allow researchers to book lab space and recruit participants. You will have accessed SONA as a participant, and when you reach Levl H your account is converted to a researcher account. This will allow you to create studies, book lab space, and recruit participants for your study.

SONA Website: Log in using your @hope credentials.

How to use SONA

You can use SONA to add:

  • Standard Lab Studies
  • Multi-Part Lab Studies
  • Online Studies using Qualtrics
  • Online Studies using Pavlovia

I have created a quick start user guide to help you set up your study in the correct way, add timeslots and recruit participants to your study.

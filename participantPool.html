Psychology Participant Pool

Getting involed with Psychology Research

Are interested in taking part in our studies?

We are always looking for volunteers to help out in our research studies. If you want to make a difference and help researchers make important discoveries that will benefit others, why not sign up to our Participant Pool?

What is the Participant Pool?

The Psychology Research Participant Pool is a group of people who have agreed to be contacted about future psychology studies in the Department of Psychology at Liverpool Hope University. Liverpool Hope will retain a list of potential participants on a secure database. When a researcher in Psychology at Hope requires participants for a study, they will obtain ethical clearance to use the panel and then an email will go out to all panel members to advertise the research opportunity.

Who can join the Pool?

Anyone over the age of 18 can join.

What kind of studies might I be asked to take part in?

It can be difficult to speculate about the nature of future studies. The types of study may vary from online experiments, online questionnaires, experiments in our laboratories, and interviews of focus groups.

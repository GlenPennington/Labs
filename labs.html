Lab Information

Welcome to the Liverpool Hope University Psychology Department Lab pages. The psychology labs are used for some small group teaching, and staff/student research activities. Labs are available for student dissertation projects. The psychology labs are located on the Ground Floor of the Health Sciences Building. They are purpose built rooms that allow researchers to develop and run experiments. Students can carry out psychological investigations using the equipment and resources described on this site. Collecting a wide range of data using these resources gives students the opportunity to enhance their psychological methods and analysis skills as well as experience complex psychological research. Equally important for your training in psychology is participation in research studies which you can access via SONA.
